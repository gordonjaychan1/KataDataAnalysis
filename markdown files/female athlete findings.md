Athlete Spotlight

* Female kata this season was defined by a close rivalry at the top between two clear frontrunners: Grace Lau (Hong Kong) and Maho Ono (Japan), both of whom competed at all 9 tournaments.
* Lau had higher peaks: she posted a higher maximum score (9.22 vs Ono's 8.88) and a higher win rate (91.7% vs 88.5%).
* Ono, on the other hand, was the more consistent of the two: she surpassed Lau in average score (8.44 vs 8.43) and median score (8.49 vs 8.40), and her scores were tighter overall, with a higher minimum (8.1 vs 7.9) and a much smaller range (0.78 vs 1.32).
* Their medal hauls reflect this balance: Lau won 5 gold, 1 silver, and 3 bronze, while Ono won 3 gold, 5 silver, and 1 bronze.
* In short, Lau reached higher peaks, but Ono was the more consistent performer across the season.
